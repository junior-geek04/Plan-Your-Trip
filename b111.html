DELIVERING HOPE

Jun 14, 2019

Logistics- it has one of the most crucial jobs all times, i.e. to connect people. We make relationships maybe between couple, between father-son or between friends. We are here to deliver joy during occasions, deliver surprises to someone who you love and deliver opportunities. But with all that we also have one more thing to do, which is to deliver hope to the survivors of natural disasters. Logistics providers are also here to deliver hope.

Logistics serves the human civilization in its best possible way on daily basis. But on the other side, logistics play a very crucial role when it comes to relief operations in disaster management. These relief operation mainly incorporate delivery of essential goods such as medicines, cloths, towels, food, utensils and first-aid kits.

Be it typhoon Idai in Africa on March 2019, floods in Kerala in August 2019 or cyclone Hagibis in Japan on October 2019. They are just to name a few. Overall more than 375 natural disaster take place on Earth each year. Many of the major parts are devastatingly affected each year on the planet. One thing that comes in favours for us is our soldier/rescuers, our Government/ NGOs and our logistics providers always trying to help the people not just survive but also thrive out of it.

The procurement, production, distribution and disposal of emergency supplies is a crucial life-saving coordination within the groups. It ensures to become a bridge between the aid material and areas of disasters becoming a key component in saving many different life-forms.
