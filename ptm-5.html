TRIP OVERVIEW

The interaction between a child and his/her parents and the child’s teacher will be an informal one in the space of an outdoor location and not in the confined spaces of a classroom. This trip would not only help and cater the interaction between the students, their parents and the teachers but would also help the students take a break from their hectic schedules and just enjoy themselves for some time.

SELF-ESTEEM is all about believing in oneself and having a positive thought about oneself. Self-esteem and self-confidence are really important for a person to walk forward in their lives. It is important for a person to be able to believe in themselves and the things that they are doing even if no one else believes in them. Having a high self-esteem also means that the person always looks at the things and challenges positively and would work hard to overcome those. Having both of these traits is important for a student. While studying in Schools and Colleges may be a little easy to deal with, the world outside of those is pretty harsh and always tries to bring a person down. Constant criticism, judgement of others may affect a student negatively if they do not have high self-esteem. They won’t be able to tackle situations if they are affected by everything that other people may say. To shape a better and brighter future for them, it is the responsibility of the parents and the teachers to inculcate the quality of self-esteem in the student and to teach them to believe in themselves.

By attending the organized PTMs, the teachers and the parents get a chance to understand a student’s mindset and collectively decide as to which approach should be taken for motivating the students to believe in themselves irrespective of the fact if others believe in them or not.

LOCATION:

These trips are conducted in collaboration with Schools, Colleges and Educational Institutions and are organized usually within a distance of 100-200 Kilometers from the pickup location.
