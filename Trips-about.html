Being a nation-wide pioneer in travel industry, we offer handcrafted trips for student as per their courses and for corporate groups. At ASKMETRIP, we wish to revolutionize our education system up to an extent where learning becomes the new ‘FUN’.

We are providing highly customized, well secured and totally amazing travel experience. We, generally customize trip for students, parents & teachers. We pride ourselves on organizing high quality, cost effective and most importantly fun-loving travel education experiences.

But we can’t hold ourselves to just that.

We, at ASKMETRIP believes in the unique fusion of globetrotting with a fun filled learning experience. We dare to elevate the basic essence of learning inside of a bubble and give you a chance to learn outside with nature itself. We believe, learning should not be limited to the classrooms, rather it should also develop an understanding of art, literature, cultural tradition and natural resources. VISION : Learn about yourself, culture, tradition in your surrounding and an empathetic attitude towards others in society.

We facilitate 5 kinds of trips

1. Fun Learn Trips : In such trip, we focus on learning from the natural environment and surroundings. We organise visit to different geographies where students can interact with different kind of natural species. This increase their knowledge base. Also, we organise group activities and teach the importance of team dynamics. This gives students a broad learning opportunity along with fun activity. We engage our students to visit a place and learn about it.

2. Curriculum Trips : Many students understand the subject when they experience it in practical manner. So, we arrange trips to Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) centres, Museum and other such places where a vast learning scope is there. These types of subject trips would be organised according to school’s requirement.

3. Expert Trips : We give a chance to our people to interact with our experts who share their learning to bring out best in you.

4. Parent-Teacher Meetrips : Teachers and Parents spend their day together, learning the best way for the overall development of their own kids.
