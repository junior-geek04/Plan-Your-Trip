HOW LOGISTICS PROVIDERS FIGHT COVID-19

Apr 19, 2020

Concerned with the very recent and furious attacks that the World is fighting against nature itself. Covid-19 has just swept the globe has rigorously attacked on human life, world economics and our medical growth.

There is a certain lack of supply and demand as well as disruption to air and sea freight. But still, global logistics is trying to pick up the pieces and re-establish the vital connection between World of trade and commerce. A Logistics service providers jobs doesn’t ends here. It is just the beginning. The job of getting safety equipment and test kits out of covid-19 hotspots to delivery of medication and sanitizers in remote areas, the task falls on the shoulder of logistics firms.

Many logistic firms like UPS, provided free air transportation services in areas like Wuhan (China). These logistics firms are supporting the delivery of medicines, protective suits, masks, medical gloves and tools, food and wheat, and most importantly hope and faith.

In India too, many logistics companies like Askmetrip.com are constantly in search of people and groups who, need any sort of transportation facility that too at minimal prices.

During this time of crises, we all must dare to fight corona by maintaining social distancing and consulting a doctor as soon as we find any symptoms of this diseases.

“We are on the frontline of this scourge. We are Logistics Company, so we are used to being nimble- we respond to hurricanes and disasters everywhere. We are always ready”

-Mr Wheeler
