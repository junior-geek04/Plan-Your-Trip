TRIP OVERVIEW

This curriculum trip is designed in such a way to train the students in agricultural science and its allied areas. The trip is all about applying concepts of science, technology, chemistry and biology to boost agricultural productivity. This curriculum trip is designed as per their current courses and exams with workshop and other live activities. This package is focusing to develop the understanding and confronting the impacts of global capitalist development on agrarian structures and environmental systems. Participants will have developed a critical understanding of key processes facing the agricultural and rural sectors and the livelihoods of rural people, as well as the structures and processes that influence sustainable development.

This trip is suitable for professionals and students who have worked on or are interested in agricultural change, rural development and environmental issues. They can be recent graduates, or come from international development agencies, journalists, community-based workers, and agrarian-environmental activists.
