EDUCATIONAL PLACES TO VISIT IN INDIA

Mar 17, 2019

People often think of Travel and Education as two mutually exclusive events. However, both of these processes and the things learnt from them are inter related to one another. People gain new experiences and learn new things from Travel. It not only improves your vocabulary, learning skills and social interaction but also helps a person be more aware of the world around them. Travelling lets you gain new experiences and learn certain things that’d be beneficial in your life. It is also important from a break point of view for students and teachers who have been working hard each day.

Students have a lot on their plate right from the studies to exams to viva and evaluations. It is important for them to take a break once in a while to just destress and relax their minds. Doing so would not only improve their health but will also make their learning and retaining skills. There are also various other methods than the typical textbook learning ways that can help to impart and gain knowledge. One of the most effective out of them is learning through Travel. Some of the benefits of travelling for the students are as mentioned:

1. Building Compassion

2. Gaining Confidence

3. More Networking

4. Inculcating Independence and Growth, and many more

There are a number of places with a high educational significance in India as well as around the world. Visiting and learning from these places can be beneficial to all the Students. We at ASKMETRIP help to organise a number of School and College field trips for students that can help to enhance their learning experience as well. We have a non-traditional approach to learning that involves a number of fun activities and games that are based on educational topics and genres. This can give the students a fun way to take a break from the traditional studying style and also help them learn some new things. Some of the places in India that have a high educational value are as mentioned:

1. Agra

2. Aurangabad

3. Chittorgarh

4. Delhi NCR

5. Jaipur

6. Jodhpur

7. Nalanda, and many more
