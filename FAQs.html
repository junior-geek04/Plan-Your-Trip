2. WHAT IS CURRICULUM WORKSHOP?

It is a hands-on workshop that lets students learn and enhance their skills. 4. WHAT IS A PARENT TEACHER MEET TRIP?

Parent teacher meets have been going in school curriculum so that the Parent can know about the progress of their child in school. We, at ASKMETRIP give a fun way to PTM meetings by organising a trip in which Parents and Teachers can interact with each other in a place apart from school. It focuses on redefining PTM interactions, encourages frank behaviour of students toward their teachers and parents, helps build trust between students-teachers and students-parents.
