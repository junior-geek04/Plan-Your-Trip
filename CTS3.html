TRIP OVERVIEW

It is a great initiative by CBSE to introduce a disaster management courses and make the students more aware of their current environment and how they can use them for their own advantage during bad times. Trying to give a small contribution in making the ideas of this subject more relevant and practical, ask me trip, bring you our curriculum trip for disaster management, which provides you the lessons of this essential subject in the most fun-loving enjoyable and practical way. This trip will not only make them grasp the concepts of the subject but will make them fall in love with it.

Disaster management courses are of utter importance because they help you to find out best ways to get out and save yourself and other out of any bad situation. This trip will be well facilitated with customized learning, providing you with detailed analysis of practical knowledge of the student in disaster management and how it can be improved. All of these real life situation define the idea that disaster management is not a mere subject, but is a way of coming out of tough situations with ease and you need to have a practical and deep understanding of it as well. During this trip, we will be learning about the same ideas and how they can be improvised.
