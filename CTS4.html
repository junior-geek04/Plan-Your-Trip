TRIP OVERVIEW

We have always been adapting to and following the same way of learning and absorbing knowledge in our educational curriculum. It has always been about learning through textbooks in a classroom with a bunch of your classmates. Although it has proven to be effective for most of the students, it may not be an easy and approachable way for some students. Every student has their own way of learning and retaining their gained knowledge and it doesnâ€™t always have to be the method of memorising the things learned in a classroom.

There can be a number of ways in which a student can learn, retain and reproduce what they have understood during their learning lessons. These ways don't always have to be the one with textbooks, it can also be in a fun way that imbibes excitement to learn in a student and one such way is by attending workshops. We at ASKMETRIP, help to organise and conduct a number of education curriculum related workshops that can be beneficial to the students.

The organised workshop will have a number of activities as well including MCQs, Quizzes or Questionnaires that could help them retain whatever they learnt during the workshop. A workshop can also be organised for more than one curriculum at the same time. For example, a combined workshop can be organised for the curriculum of History and Civics. The subjects of collaboration can depend on the educational curriculums of the students and a plan can be chalked out accordingly.
