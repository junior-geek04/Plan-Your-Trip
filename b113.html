INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT GLOBAL LOGISTICS MARKET

Oct 16, 2019

FACT: 1

Despite all the buzzing of Drones and airplanes, trucks are still the most dominant nearly 70% of freight transport in US is handled by trucks. In fact, trucks transport $671 billion worth of manufacturing each year. Every one out of fifteen people are engaged in trucking and logistics.

FACT: 2

Companies that use smart plans and logistics strategies like outsourcing. These companies are able to save up to 13%.

FACT: 3

There are 531 industrial robots for every 10,000 employees in South Korea making it World’s highest density robots.

FACT: 4

The World’s largest shipping containers are so large that they can carry an Eiffel tower and an airbus comfortably. According to Plunkett Research, transportation sector alone is the world’s fastest growing sector with revenue of 5.1 trillion worldwide.

FACT: 5

Many Asian countries such as China and India are on their path to become global Logistics hotspots. The whole South-east Asia has more than 150% trade to GDP ratio.

FACT: 6

Same day shipping is becoming a new normal. More than 65% of retailers promise to provide same day shipping facilities.

FACT: 7

(Radio Frequency Identification) RFID is changing supply chains worldwide. Implementation of RFID tags has made inventory accuracy as high as 95 %. It has now become a lot easier for worker to keep track of goods.

FACT: 8

Freighting is now a days, a major cause of air pollution. Ke3eping that in consideration, trucks which used for shipment are now being turned into eco-friendly trucks, making them more efficient and less energy consumer.

FACT: 9

About 90% of global logistics start-ups take place in USA and Asia, whereas in Europe, investments reach up to just 5%.

FACT: 10

Artificial Programming Interface (API) Artificial Intelligence and Block Chains are three newer technologies which promise to expand global logistics market forever.
