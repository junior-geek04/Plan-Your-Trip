ABOUT THE TRIP

At ASKMETRIP, we believe that team stands for:

T- Together    E- Everyone    A- Achieves    M- More

So, this trip majorly focuses on team building activities and how can a group of people can a group of people with different talents, abilities, experiences and backgrounds come together for a shared purpose, make that purpose evolve and evolve with that purpose. This one day trip can bring your team back in action with each other sharing new ideas, making new friends and forgetting past experiences. Our experts and their techniques, ideas and experiences at the trip will make this learning, much more fun-filled and enjoyable, so that learning becomes our new ‘FUN’.

TOPIC DESCRIPTION

A team is not only a group of people working together and we understand that. This trip if full of such exciting activities that would re-define the meaning of team. This trip will be well facilitated with customized learning, providing you with detailed analysis of your personality and team building skills and how they can be improved. This team building training trip will build confidence, humbleness, cooperation, adaptability and most important of all trust and care within your team. This will help them to overcome their individual differences and become one to accomplish one common goal. Not only this, but it will also give a new workstyle to your work place.
