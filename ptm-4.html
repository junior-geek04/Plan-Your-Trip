TRIP OVERVIEW

The interaction between a child and his/her parents and the child’s teacher will be an informal one in the space of an outdoor location and not in the confined spaces of a classroom. This trip would not only help and cater the interaction between the students, their parents and the teachers but would also help the students take a break from their hectic schedules and just enjoy themselves for some time.

DISCIPLINE is an important trait that should be inculcated in a student’s life. By being disciplined, one can achieve great things and their set goals. Discipline brings along a sense of responsibility along with it. It teaches a person to own up to their actions and take up the opportunity of improving themselves. It is important for a student to be disciplined in his or her life as well. However, in order to imbibe discipline in them, punishments and beatings were used. There can be other friendly and optimistic ways in which discipline can be imbibed in students. Punishments just create a fear amongst them which may affect their health and their curriculum performance negatively. The alternative ways to discipline a child include providing them with a timeout, ensuring that they are heard, asking mundane questions and many others. By attending the organized PTMs, the teachers and the parents get a chance to understand a student’s mindset and collectively decide as to which approach should be taken for disciplining them.

LOCATION:

These trips are conducted in collaboration with Schools, Colleges and Educational Institutions and are organized usually within a distance of 100-200 Kilometers from the pickup location.
